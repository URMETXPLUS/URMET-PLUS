ONYX
The new concept in access control

Onyx is Urmet's new IP entry panel with a 10" touchscreen display and facial recognition function, designed to ensure the highest level of security in access control.

Design and technology total black

With its clean lines, total black finish, and large touchscreen display, the new ONYX entry panel is a symbol of elegance and simplicity. Advanced IP technology, different input control modes, and two-factor authentication redefine access control security standards.

ONYX elevates access protection

  • Facial recognition: biometric, secure, intelligent. Each user creates their own profile: one account, one face.
  • Two-way authentication: Combining facial biometric recognition with a personal code or QR code for maximum security.

Smartphone management with the CallMe Uin app

The Urmet CallMe Uin app allows you to manage guests and external access from your smartphone in complete security. Generate temporary, personalized passes for visitors in different modes to suit every need.

Guest Management

Invite with Ease

Grant temporary access to your visitors through our secure digital invitation system.

1

Start Invitation

Open the URMET PLUS app as a resident and select the Send Invitation option.

2

Visitor Details

Input the visitor's information, set the arrival date and time, then tap the Submit button to generate the request.

3

Share Link

Simply Copy the Link generated by the app and send it to your guest via your preferred messaging platform.

4

Guest Entry

The visitor opens the link to receive their Invitation QR Code, ready to be scanned at the entry gate.

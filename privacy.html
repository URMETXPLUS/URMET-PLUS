1. Data Collection / 數據收集

We collect information necessary to provide access control services, including your name, contact details, and encrypted QR credential data used for building entry.

2. Data Usage / 數據用途

Your data is used solely for identity verification, facility booking, and ensuring building security. We do not sell your personal information.
您的數據僅用於身份驗證、設施預約及確保大廈安全。我們不會出售您的個人資料。

3. Data Security / 數據安全

URMET PLUS uses high-level encryption to protect your digital credentials. Access logs are stored securely and only accessible by authorized administrators.

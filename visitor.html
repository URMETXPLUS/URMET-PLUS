Walk-in & Delivery Access

Instant Online Registration

Enabling food deliverers, couriers, and walk-in visitors to register securely in seconds without physical contact or paperwork.

1

Online Registration

Visitors or delivery staff scan the QR code posted at the entrance to open the digital registration form on their mobile device.

2

SMS Verification

For enhanced security, a One Time Password (OTP) is sent via SMS to the visitor's phone for immediate identity verification.

3

Secure Entry

Once verified, the visitor receives an access code. Present it to the Multi-Reader and the gate will open automatically.
